--- title: "Modeling and analysis workflows for energy systems" excerpt: "Reproducible engineering workflows for simulation, evaluation, and communication of power-system research results." collection: portfolio ---

This work packages simulation, analysis, and communication tasks into cleaner engineering workflows for research in energy systems. It helps turn technical studies into reusable results that can be shared with collaborators, presented clearly, and maintained over time.

The outcome is more consistent project execution and faster iteration across modeling, validation, and dissemination tasks.
