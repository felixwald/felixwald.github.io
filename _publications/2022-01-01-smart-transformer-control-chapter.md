Book chapter covering Smart Transformer (Solid-State Transformer) control strategies for managing the electrical grid. Topics include frequency regulation, voltage support, reactive power compensation, and power flow management enabled by the SST's power electronic interface. The chapter places SST control within the broader context of modern power system stability and control theory.
