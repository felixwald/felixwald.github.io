A comprehensive critical review of the current and prospective applications of Solid-State Transformers (SSTs) in active distribution networks. The paper systematically surveys SST services across four domains: power quality improvement, grid support (frequency and voltage regulation), energy management, and system protection. It analyses 182 references and provides a structured assessment of the technical readiness, benefits, and limitations of SST deployment in modern distribution grids with high penetrations of renewable energy sources and inverter-based resources.
