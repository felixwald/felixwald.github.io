Lab course as part of the M.Sc. Electrical Engineering programme at KIT. Students learn real-time simulation and hardware-in-the-loop testing using professional power electronics testing equipment.

**Topics covered:**
- Real-time simulation with OPAL-RT
- Power Hardware-in-the-Loop (PHIL) methodology
- Grid-connected converter control
- Testing and validation of power electronic systems

**Tools:** OPAL-RT, Imperix, Egston, MATLAB/Simulink
