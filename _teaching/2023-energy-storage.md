Tutorial and exercise course covering energy storage technologies and their integration into electrical power systems. Responsible for leading exercises and tutorials, guiding students through both the technology fundamentals and system-level integration challenges.

**Role:** Leading exercise and tutorial sessions.

**Topics covered:**
- Overview of energy storage technologies
- Compressed Air Energy Storage (CAES)
- Gravity-based storage systems
- Chemical storage: batteries (electrochemical fundamentals, cell chemistry, aging)
- Hydrogen: electrolysis, fuel cells, power-to-gas
- System integration of storage in power networks
- Load demand curves
- Frequency response and grid stability with high storage penetration
