Course for mechanical engineering students covering the fundamentals of electrical engineering. Responsible for leading tutorials and designing exercise sessions.

**Role:** Leading tutorials and exercise sessions.

**Topics covered:**
- DC circuit analysis: network analysis, mesh/node methods
- Superposition principle
- Coils and inductors
- Alternating current (AC) circuits: phasors, impedance, power
- DC machines
- Transformers
- Induction machines
- Synchronous and AC machines
- Operational amplifiers
- Semiconductors and diodes
- Power electronics fundamentals
