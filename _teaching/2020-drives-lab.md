Teaching assistance for the undergraduate/graduate lab course on electrical drives and power electronics at KIT.

**Topics covered:**
- DC and AC motor drives
- Power electronic converters (AC/DC, DC/DC)
- FPGA-based converter control
- Converter design and PCB layout

**Tools:** FPGAs, C, VHDL, Matlab, Altium, Quartus, Vivado
