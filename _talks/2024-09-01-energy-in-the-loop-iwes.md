Talk presented at the 7th International Workshop on Grid Simulator Testing of Energy Systems and Wind Turbine Drivetrains, hosted by Fraunhofer IWES in Bremen.

The presentation introduced the Energy-in-the-Loop (EiL) concept as practiced at the KIT Energy Lab, covering the spectrum from pure grid simulation through Controller HIL (CHIL) to high-power Power Hardware-in-the-Loop (PHIL) testing — and how these methods enable realistic testing of power electronic systems under grid conditions.
